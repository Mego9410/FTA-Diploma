Sound familiar?

Everything tells you to buy a practice. Nothing tells you how.

You're an excellent dentist. But ownership is a different skill set — and the gap between "associate" and "principal" is exactly where most ambitious dentists get stuck.

“

I don't know what a practice is actually worth

Goodwill, EBITDA, multiples, NHS vs private mix — the numbers feel like a foreign language, and you're terrified of overpaying.

“

Funding and deal structures are a black box

How much deposit? Which lender? Asset vs share purchase? Earn-outs? You don't know what "good" looks like — or what to avoid.

“

Due diligence scares me

You've heard the horror stories — CQC issues, dodgy leases, hidden liabilities. You don't know which landmines to look for.

“

I've never actually run a business

Payroll, compliance, HR, marketing, P&L. Clinical school never taught this, and the responsibility feels overwhelming.

“

I feel too inexperienced to negotiate

You'll be sitting across from seasoned sellers, accountants and solicitors. How do you hold your own and protect your interests?

“

What if I get it wrong?

This is the biggest financial decision of your life. One expensive mistake could set you back years — so you keep putting it off.

This diploma exists for exactly this moment. We turn "one day" into a clear, guided plan.

The curriculum

Eight modules. One confident buyer.

Each module builds on the last, ending in a live final project on buying a real dental practice — so you finish with applied skills, not just notes.

01

Buying a practice

The full buying journey, the market today, and how to find and assess the right opportunity for you.

02

Financial planning

Personal and practice finances, affordability, cashflow and building the financial foundation for ownership.

03

Funding & deal structures

Lenders, deposits, asset vs share purchases, earn-outs and structuring a deal that protects you.

04

Legal framework & due diligence

Contracts, leases, CQC, NHS contracts and the diligence checklist that uncovers the risks before you sign.

05

Valuation & negotiation skills

How practices are really valued — and how to negotiate confidently to the right price and terms.

06

Transition from old to new owner

Handover, staff, patients and systems — managing the changeover smoothly and protecting goodwill.

07

Running the practice from day one

People, performance, marketing, systems and leading a team — managing the business from day one.

08

Risk, compliance, governance & scaling

Governance and compliance done right — plus how to scale and build a multi-site portfolio over time.

★
Final project

A live project on buying a dental practice

Pull it all together into a real-world buying project — pressure-tested by your tutors and mentors — so you walk away ready to act.

Questions

Everything you might be wondering

Is the diploma accredited?
This is a structured, recognised qualification rather than just an in-house certificate. We're finalising the full accreditation details and will confirm them for the January 2027 cohort.
How much does it cost?
The diploma is £9,000 + VAT in total — one inclusive fee covering the complete 8-month programme, including all face-to-face sessions, the qualification and your final project. 0% finance is available over 9 months, working out at £1,000 per month + VAT.
How is it delivered?
Eight modules across eight months, with a face-to-face meeting in London each month. Between sessions you'll work through applied case material, culminating in a live final project on buying a dental practice.
Do I need to be ready to buy right now?
No. The diploma is designed for ambitious dentists at the "I want to, but I'm not sure how" stage. Whether you plan to buy in 12 months or are still exploring, you'll finish ready and confident.
What happens after I finish?
You receive ongoing mentoring, and if you go on to buy a practice within two years of completing, we provide hands-on support as you buy — backed by the wider FTA network.
Who is teaching it?
The programme is led by Dr Raj Rattan MBE, alongside Andy Acton, Chris Strevens, Davesh Patel, Vic Gethani and specialist guest tutors who are leaders in finance, law, valuation and practice operations.
What does registering my interest commit me to?
Nothing. Registering simply gives you priority access and a personal call to learn more. There's no obligation to enrol.
